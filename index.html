Auroracoin is a cryptocurrency launched in 2014 for primary use within the nation of Iceland.

In May 2018 the Auroracoin Foundation merged into The Icelandic Blockchain Foundation bringing needed exposure to a larger population of business and technology interests in Iceland. In many ways 2018 promises a re-launch of this unique use-case coin.

Looking Ahead

Here in Iceland we are excited about the new Iceland Blockchain Foundation and recent developments in Blockchain regulation. More merchants are accepting Auroracoin and general interest, involvement, and comprehension has been on the rise.
